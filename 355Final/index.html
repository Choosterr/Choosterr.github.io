Are Newer Chapions Always Relevant?

Are New Champs Must-Picks?

Since Worlds 2016, there have been 42 new champions added to League of Legends. However, how often do they appear in Worlds following their release? This tool aims to filter and visualize newer champions playable during their launch year of international competition.

How Much do Patch Notes Affect Champions Picked?

Do Buffs or Nerfs Matter?

League of Legends is a constantly changing game. From new champion reworks to item changes, there are many numbers being adjusted in an effort to balance the game. This visualization showcases how balance changes to champions during the World's patch affects their tournament statistics.
